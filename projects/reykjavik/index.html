The Reykjavik Index for Leadership

The Reykjavik Index for Leadership measures the extent to which men and women are viewed equally in terms of the suitability of individuals for positions of power. The Index runs from 0 to 100; a score of 100 means that across society, there is complete agreement that men and women are equally suited to leadership in all sectors.
